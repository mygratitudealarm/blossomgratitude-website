What is gratitude, really

The difference between knowing and feeling

Gratitude is the ability to fully feel appreciation for something. In your body, not just in your mind.

Thinking "I'm grateful for my family"
Feeling Actually feeling warmth, connection, emotion

Gratitude isn't a thought. It's a feeling.
And most people have never trained that feeling.
